ACTIONS

Actions can be added by clicking the Add Action button or selecting the action from the dropdown list.

When a schedule needs to be handled by the schedule manager, its actions are executed sequentially in the order represented by the schedules subtree.

The schedule manager ignores all unchecked items and any children of unchecked items.

Under certain circumstances, an action can "FAIL" which tells the manager to ignore all of the action's children.
